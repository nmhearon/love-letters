Dear GitHub,

I have to say, I felt a little strange having to make an online account, but it was all worth it to get to you. Although we met online, you and I quickly became friends locally. One of the first things we did together was create a repository to share all of our information together. And now that I know how well we fit together, I'm constantly commiting to you. All I have to do is git push all of my new memories and information to our repository and by simply entering my username and password, you accept everything I have to say. And while online dating isn't always the coolest option, somehow I feel like it's the only way I would've wanted to be with you. But a lot of people can't always understand that concept, and that why we have our README.me. And while I must admit that I was hesitant to learn how to speak in markdown language, it was really easy to grasp and I'm thankful I did. I'm looking forward to a long and collaborative future together 

<3
